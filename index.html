Step-by-step guide

1

Step 1: Registration

Click on the signup button if you haven't registered before.
Your email, phone number, a username, and bvn is required.
Your information is safe with us.
Tap on register once your information is confirmed.

2

Step 2: Choose a plan

After successful registeration, tap on plan on the nav bar.
Choose your preferred plan and fill the commitment form.
Submit
A congratulatory mail will be sent with a unique account number for your ajo payment.

3

Step 3: Payment method

After successful commitment, you will be redirected to method of payment
Follow the step by step guide to link your bank account.
Every due payment will be deducted from this account on due date.

4

Step 4: Reward day

On due date, your payment will be automatically sent to your registered bank account.
